Worldwide, DMX sold over 74 million records, and his albums were certified platinum 14 times in the United States. He was also nominated for a Grammy Award three times.

His most successful songs are:

Party Up

Where the Hood At

X Gon' Give It To Ya
  • 1998: It's Dark and Hell Is Hot
  • 1998: Flesh of My Flesh, Blood of My Blood
  • 1998: And Then There Was X
  • 2001: The Great Depression
  • 2003: Grand Champ
  • 2006: Year of the Dog… Again
  • 2012: Undisputed
  • 2021: Exodus

In addition to these albums, DMX released an impressive number of 89 singles & EPs as well as 18 compilations. For a complete overview of his discography, please visit his Discogs page.
